# Project 2B pathway enrichment summary

Comparison: ER_pos breast tumors vs TNBC breast tumors

Differential expression direction:
- Positive log2FoldChange = higher expression in ER_pos tumors
- Negative log2FoldChange = higher expression in TNBC tumors

Significant DE genes:
- FDR cutoff: padj < 0.05
- Total significant DE genes: 3674
- Higher in ER_pos: 1778
- Higher in TNBC: 1896

Significant protein-coding DE genes:
- Total: 3280
- Higher in ER_pos: 1515
- Higher in TNBC: 1765

GO Biological Process enrichment:

ER_pos-up protein-coding genes were enriched for biological processes related to hormone regulation, hormone transport/secretion, signal release, and cilium/microtubule-associated organization.

TNBC-up protein-coding genes were enriched for biological processes related to mitotic division, nuclear division, chromosome segregation, sister chromatid segregation, and organelle fission.

Interpretation:
These results suggest that ER_pos-up genes reflect hormone/luminal-associated transcriptional programs, while TNBC-up genes show a strong proliferative/cell-cycle-associated transcriptional signature.

Caution:
These enrichment results indicate overrepresentation of GO Biological Process terms among subtype-associated gene sets. They do not prove causal pathway activation.
