# Cohort Selection

## Research Question

Which genes and biological pathways distinguish
ER-positive breast cancer from Triple Negative Breast Cancer?

---

## Dataset

GSE58135

Human bulk RNA-seq breast cancer dataset.

Contains:
- ER+ primary tumors
- TNBC primary tumors

---

## Experimental Design

Group 1:
ER+ Breast Cancer

Group 2:
Triple Negative Breast Cancer (TNBC)

---

## Planned Cohort Size

ER+:
10 biological replicates

TNBC:
10 biological replicates

Total:
20 tumor samples

---

## Inclusion Criteria

- Human breast tumor samples
- RNA-seq data available
- Clearly annotated subtype
- Primary tumor samples

---

## Exclusion Criteria

- Normal tissue samples
- Cell lines
- Samples with unclear subtype annotation
